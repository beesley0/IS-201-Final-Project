Places we have skied:

  1. Niseko United Resort, Japan
    • After over a day travelling, we arrived at Niseko United where nearly 15 inches of snow fall everyday.
    • We had delicious food and enjoyed learning more about Japanese culture.

  2. Targhee Resort, Wyoming
    • Popular location for our family reunions with my Grandpa who lives in Idaho.
    • High level skiing, however, there are also many opportunities for beginners.

  3. Alta Resort, Utah
    • Our favorite resort close to home. It's high altitude guarantees large amounts of snowfall every year.
    • Spring skiing lasts until mid-May.
